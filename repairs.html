01 — Common problems we fix

Aapki problem
is list mein honi chahiye.

Aur agar nahi hai — toh bhi le aao. Humne aaj tak jo nahi dekha, kal dekh lenge. 40 saal ka tajurba khali nahi hai.

Television

LED · LCD · OLED · Smart TV

  • Picture nahi aa rahi, screen blank
  • Sound aa raha par picture nahi
  • Display mein crack ya lines
  • Dark patches ya flickering
  • Remote kaam nahi karta
  • HDMI / USB port loose
  • Apps open nahi ho rahe (Smart TV)

Air Conditioner

Split · Window · Inverter

  • Cooling nahi kar raha
  • Awaaz aa rahi ya vibration
  • Paani leak ho raha
  • Gas refill / top-up chahiye
  • Remote / mode issue
  • Bijli zyada kha raha
  • Installation / shifting service

Microwave Oven

Solo · Grill · Convection

  • Heat nahi kar raha
  • Plate ghoom nahi rahi
  • Spark / smoke aa raha
  • Door theek se band nahi hota
  • Display blank ya error
  • Buttons kaam nahi karte
  • Light nahi jal rahi

Sound Systems

Speaker · Soundbar · Home Theatre

  • Speaker phat raha / crackle
  • Sound aa raha par halki
  • Bluetooth pair nahi ho raha
  • One side se sound nahi
  • Subwoofer dead
  • AUX / HDMI input issue
  • Power on nahi ho raha

Kitchen Appliances

Mixer · Grinder · Induction · Toaster

  • Mixer motor jam ya weak
  • Grinder se bahut awaaz
  • Induction "E0" / error code
  • Heating coil dead
  • Switch loose ya tooti
  • Power cord damaged
  • Jar tooti, blade replacement

Home Appliances

Geyser · Iron · Fan · Inverter

  • Geyser garam nahi ho raha
  • Iron heat nahi kar raha
  • Inverter beep maar raha
  • Fan speed kam ya awaaz
  • Battery backup nahi mil raha
  • Thermostat / element replace
  • Capacitor blow ho gaya

02 — The craft

Humne hazaron khole hain.
Aapka bhi sahi haathon mein hai.

Display panel

LED, OLED, LCD — sahi replacement source karte hain, "kuch bhi fit kar do" nahi.

Power & mainboard

Zyada-tar "dead TVs" ek ₹400 capacitor se theek ho jati hain. Pehle check karte hain.

Backlight strips

Dim picture, dark patches, flicker — almost always LED strips. Quick fix, proper kaam.

Speakers & ports

Crackly sound, dead HDMI, loose stand — chhote kaam ke "bade" daam nahi lete.
