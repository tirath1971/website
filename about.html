01 — Brand story

Hum kya bechte hain?
Peace of mind.

"We sell electronics, but what we really sell is peace of mind — knowing your stuff will work, and if it doesn't, someone will fix it properly."

Tirath Electronics is a neighbourhood electronics shop — the kind of place where you can buy a new TV, get an old AC repaired, and actually get a straight answer about what's wrong with it.

We serve everyone — students apna pehla headphone lene aate hain, families TV upgrade karte hain, dada-dadi 30 saal purana radio le aate hain. No talking down, no upselling, no nonsense.

Brand ka feel hamesha woh trusted rishtedaar wala hona chahiye jo electronics jaanta ho — knowledgeable, calm, aur kabhi aapko silly feel nahi karwata kuch poochne pe.

Tirath Singh

Owner · CEO · Master Technician · 40+ years

Tirath Singh ne 40+ saal pehle yeh shop shuru ki, ek simple soch ke saath — ki neighbourhood ko ek aisa electronics wala chahiye jo seedhi baat kare, asli kaam kare, aur har customer ko apne ghar wale ki tarah treat kare.

Aaj bhi har repair khud check karte hain, har customer ko personally greet karte hain, aur har quote khud likhte hain. Generations of families in Mukerian have brought their broken TVs, dead ACs, sparking microwaves to him — kyunki "Tirath bhaiya" ka naam hi bharosa hai.
